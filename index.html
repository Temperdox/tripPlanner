üìÖ Trip Itinerary

Day 1-2

Tokyo Arrival & Exploration

Land at Narita/Haneda, check into Henn na Hotel Maihama. Explore Tokyo Disney area and nearby Ikspiari shopping mall.

Day 3-4

Central Tokyo Adventures

Shibuya Crossing, Harajuku, Meiji Shrine. Visit Don Quijote Shibuya store. Explore Akihabara electronics district and Yodobashi Akiba.

Day 5

Traditional Tokyo

Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, traditional shopping street, and explore traditional Japanese culture.

Day 6-7

Cat Island Adventure

Travel to Aoshima "Cat Island" or Tashirojima via ferry. Two nights experiencing cat paradise and traditional Japanese accommodation.

Day 8-9

Kyoto Cultural Immersion

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Gion District. Traditional kaiseki dinner and possible geisha spotting.

Day 10

Nara Day Trip

Visit Nara Park, feed the sacred deer, explore Todaiji Temple with the Great Buddha, and Kasuga Taisha Shrine.

Day 11-12

Osaka Food & Fun

Dotonbori district, Universal Studios Japan, Osaka Castle. Famous takoyaki and okonomiyaki food tours.

Day 13

Mount Fuji & Hot Springs

Hakone region, Lake Kawaguchi, onsen experience. Weather permitting: Mount Fuji climbing or scenic viewing.

Day 14

Final Tokyo Shopping & Departure

Last-minute shopping at Don Quijote, souvenir hunting in Asakusa, departure preparations.

Week 1

Tokyo Deep Dive

Comprehensive Tokyo exploration: all major districts, neighborhoods, and attractions. Shibuya, Harajuku, Akihabara, Asakusa, Ginza, Roppongi. Include Yodobashi Akiba and multiple Don Quijote visits.

Week 2

Kansai Region Adventure

Extended time in Kyoto (temples, bamboo forest, traditional districts), Osaka (food culture, Universal Studios), Nara Park with deer feeding and temple visits.

Week 3

Hidden Gems & Nature

Cat islands (Aoshima or Tashirojima), Mount Fuji region, Hakone hot springs, Nikko temples, lesser-known destinations and local experiences.

Week 4

Regional Exploration

Choose from: Hiroshima & Miyajima, Kanazawa, Takayama, or northern regions. Extended cultural immersion and unique local experiences.

Final Days

Tokyo Farewell Tour

Return to Tokyo for final shopping, revisiting favorite spots, last-minute experiences, and departure preparations.

üí° Essential Tips

üé´ Transportation

JR Pass recommended for longer trips. IC cards (Suica/Pasmo) for local transport. Book Shinkansen seats in advance during peak seasons.

üí¥ Money Matters

Japan is still largely cash-based. Withdraw from 7-Eleven ATMs. Many places don't accept foreign cards. Budget ¬•10,000-15,000 cash per day.

üì± Connectivity

Rent pocket WiFi or get SIM card at airport. Download Google Translate with camera feature. Hyperdia app for train schedules.

üçú Food Culture

Don't tip (it's insulting). Slurp noodles (it's polite). Try conveyor belt sushi, ramen shops, and convenience store food!

üéå Cultural Etiquette

Bow when greeting. Remove shoes indoors. Don't eat while walking. Be quiet on trains. No loud phone conversations.

üõçÔ∏è Shopping Strategy

Tax-free shopping available for tourists. Don Quijote for quirky items. Department store basement food courts are amazing. 100-yen shops for basics.
